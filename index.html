Information

Daycare and Boarding

The purpose of The Doggy Depot is to provide a safe, fun and stimulating social environment for dogs. Experienced staff will be interacting and encouraging safe, appropriate interaction and play. Crates are available to provide a quiet rest time for all pets needing a break. Most dogs do well in this environment learning safe play, basic obedience and good doggy manners using positive motivation. The Doggy Depot staff will give owners a report of their pets at the end of their fun day. To ensure the safety and health of your pet and our other guests, we require all guests to comply with the following rules and regulations.

Age

All dogs should be at least 12 weeks (3 months) of age or older.

Sex

We recommend all dogs be spayed or neutered by 7 months of age. We will take dogs that are not spayed or neutered, but if inappropriate behaviors consistently occur or while a dog is in heat they will not be allowed.

Shots

All dogs must have up-to-date vaccinations. Owners must submit written proof of DHLPP (Distemper/Parvo), Bordatella (kennel cough). Rabies is required for puppies over 4 months of age.

Health

All dogs must be in good health. Owners will certify their dog(s) are in good health and have not been ill with a communicable condition. Upon admission, all dogs must be free from any condition which could potentially jeopardize other guests. Dogs that have been ill with a communicable condition in the last 30 days will require veterinarian certification of health to be admitted or readmitted. Any dogs with coughing, sneezing, nasal discharge or eye discharge will need to be symptom free for 3 days before returning to daycare and boarding. A waiting period of 2 days is required after a Bordatella vaccine is given, before the dog(s) can attend daycare or boards overnight.

Behavior

All dogs must be non-aggressive and not food or toy protective. Owners will certify their dog(s) have not harmed or shown any aggressive or threatening behavior toward any person or any other dog(s). Please remember that your pet will be spending time with other pets and the safety and health of all animals is our main concern.
